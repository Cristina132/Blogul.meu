London, the capital of the United Kingdom, boasts a rich history dating back to Roman times. The River Thames runs through its heart, adorned with iconic landmarks like the Tower of London and the Houses of Parliament. This multicultural city features world-class museums, diverse cuisine, and a vibrant theatre scene in the West End. London's extensive public transportation includes the historic Tube. Royal palaces, financial districts, and green spaces like Hyde Park contribute to its global appeal. Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the Shard are among the many landmarks that define this dynamic metropolis.

Pasta Amatriciana is a classic Italian dish from Amatrice, Lazio. It features pasta with a sauce made from guanciale (cured pork cheek), tomatoes, onions, garlic, chili flakes, and Pecorino Romano cheese. The guanciale is fried until crispy, then combined with the other ingredients to create a rich, savory, and slightly spicy sauce. It's a beloved dish in Italian cuisine, known for its bold flavors and comforting appeal.
